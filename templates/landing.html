How Does DiagnoMate Work ?

Using cutting-edge technology and medical expertise, DiagnoMate analyzes your symptoms and provides personalized recommendations tailored to your specific situation. Our user-friendly interface makes it easy to input your symptoms and receive instant feedback on possible causes and next steps.

Begin by inputting your symptoms into the DiagnoMate platform. You can describe your symptoms in detail, including their severity, duration, and any additional relevant information.DiagnoMate carefully analyzes the symptoms you've provided, cross-referencing them with its extensive medical database.

Based on the symptom analysis, DiagnoMate generates a list of potential conditions or diseases that could be causing your symptoms. This list is known as a "differential diagnosis" and is ranked based on the likelihood of each condition.

DiagnoMate then provides you with personalized recommendations based on the differential diagnosis. These recommendations may include advice on self-care measures, suggestions for further evaluation, or guidance on when to seek medical attention.
